Key Innovation:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Instead of fixed thresholds (Exp 4), calculate them dynamically per fold:
  - Analyze confidence distributions from all 7 directions
  - centered_boost = centered_mean + 0.025
  - high_conf = overall_mean + 1.0 * std
  - very_high_conf = overall_mean + 1.5 * std
This auto-calibrates for each fold's unique characteristics!
